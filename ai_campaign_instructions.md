# 🧠 AI Campaign Instructions

## 📌 Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Choose Your AI Tools
- **Images**: Use DALL·E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion
- **Videos**: Use Pictory, Synthesia, or Runway
- **Copywriting**: Use ChatGPT, Google Bard, or Copy.ai

### 2. Generate Media
- Input a descriptive prompt to AI image/video tools
- Use templates for faster results
- Export the file or get a direct link

### 3. Replace Placeholders
- Find the AI placeholder in the email template (e.g., `[[AI_IMAGE]]`)
- Insert your AI-generated image or video link
- Customize copy and layout depending on the style of the campaign

### 4. Test and Send
- Preview your email to ensure all links and images load
- Send it through your email marketing platform (e.g., Mailchimp, Constant Contact)

---

## 📌 Best Practices

- **Use high-quality sources** for AI image and video generation
- **Keep copy short and impactful** for email readability
- **Test variants** with the same brand message — AI can generate multiple versions
- **Use placeholders** in your email template to simplify maintenance and updates

---

## 📌 Optional: Automate with AI
- Use tools like Zapier or Make (formerly Integromat) to automate campaign creation
- Set up batch generation for image/video prompts in your AI tools
